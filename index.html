Man (Echo) Su

I investigate how to support students from linguistically and culturally diverse backgrounds in understanding complex STEM concepts through interactive simulations, AI-supported pedagogical agents, and educational games. I also adopt multimodal learning analytics approaches to analyze and foster self-regulated learning. My current projects focus on human-AI interaction in education, the design and evaluation of adaptive learning systems, and data-informed instructional support in STEM learning contexts.
