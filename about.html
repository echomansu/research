Dr. Man (Echo) Su

Research Group Leader | Principle Investigator

Tübingen Digital Teaching Lab (TüDiLab)

The Leibniz-Institut für Wissensmedien (IWM)

I conduct research at the intersection of learning sciences, human computer interaction, and teacher education. My work investigates how to design, implement, and evaluate digital learning and teaching environments that promote deep conceptual understanding and foster self-regulated learning in both formal and informal settings.

I'm committed to translating research into practice by building sustainable researcher-practitioner ecosystems that support the thoughtful integration of technology into learning and teaching.

I completed my Ph.D. training in Learning, Literacies, and Technologies at Arizona State University under the guidance of Prof. Michelene (Micki) Chi.
